The Corn Maze

Thirty acres of carved farmland in the wide-open Red River Valley — flat land stretching to every horizon. Five levels, from easy family trails to expert-level challenge. One of the largest corn mazes in the entire United States, and in 2026, honoring America's 250th with all five military branches.
