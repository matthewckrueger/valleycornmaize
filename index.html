Seasonal Extras

More Fall Magic to Enjoy

Flower Field

Our zinnia flowerfield becomes one of the most beautiful spots in the Red River Valley blooming during Sunflower Dayz and going into the first half of September. Bring your camera — the light in the valley at this time of year is extraordinary.

Pumpkins for Sale

Take home the perfect fall pumpkin to decorate your porch. Available during the month of October - while supplies last! Pick from a variety of sizes for you and your family to enjoy at home!
