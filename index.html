The Main Event

The Corn Maze

30 acres of twists, turns, and surprises carved into the flat Red River Valley farmland. Five levels from family-friendly to genuinely challenging. One of the largest corn mazes in the entire United States, and in 2026, a living tribute to America's 250th birthday.

Seasonal Extras

More Fall Magic to Enjoy

Flower Field

Our zinnia flowerfield becomes one of the most beautiful spots in the Red River Valley blooming during Sunflower Dayz and going into the first half of September. Bring your camera — the light in the valley at this time of year is extraordinary.

Pumpkins for Sale

Take home the perfect fall pumpkin to decorate your porch. Available during the month of October - while supplies last! Pick from a variety of sizes for you and your family to enjoy at home!

On-Site Concessions

Fuel the Adventure

Everything made fresh on-site. The mini donuts have a legendary reputation around East Grand Forks — don't leave without trying them.

Taco in a Bag
A fan favorite — seasoned taco goodness served right in the bag. Messy, filling, and worth every bite.
Mini Donuts
Hot, fresh, and the stuff of local legend. Dusted in cinnamon sugar — the smell alone will find you.
Hotdogs
A fall classic. Simple, satisfying, and always a hit with the kids after a long run through the maze.
Apple Blossoms
Warm, flaky pastry filled with spiced apple — the taste of fall in every bite. A Valley Corn Maize staple.
Pie in a Cone
A unique treat you won't find anywhere else. Creamy, sweet, and the perfect handheld fall dessert.
Cold Drinks
Sodas, water, lemonade & more. Stay refreshed all day as you explore every acre of the valley.
