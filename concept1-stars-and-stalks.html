What's Here

A Full Day of Family Fun

The corn maze is just the beginning. Valley Corn Maize is packed with thrills, activities, and fresh-made treats for every age.

The Main Event

The Corn Maze

Thirty acres of twists, turns, and surprises — five levels ranging from family-friendly to genuinely challenging. Carved into the flat Red River Valley landscape for a view like nowhere else in the country. This is one of the largest corn mazes in the entire United States, and the only one honoring America's 250th birthday.
