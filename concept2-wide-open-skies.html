The Main Event

The Corn Maze

Thirty acres of farm-carved pathways through the Red River Valley — flat, wide open land that stretches to every horizon. Five levels from easy to expert. One of the largest corn mazes in the entire United States, and in 2026, the only one honoring America's 250th birthday with all five military branches.
